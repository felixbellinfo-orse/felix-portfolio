About

Felix is a spatial designer and object maker whose work reflects on the tension between designs' anthropic origins and our inherent animality. He often embraces transdisciplinary design-research to tell stories focused on bridging the engrained divides that exist between culture and nature by giving agency to the invisible or unnoticed relationships with which we are bound in modern life.
